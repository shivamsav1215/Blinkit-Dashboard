8. Visual Analysis

- Total Sales by Fat Content (Donut Chart): Analyzes the impact of low-fat vs regular items on total sales and related KPIs.
- Total Sales by Item Type (Bar Chart): Identifies top-performing categories such as fruits, snacks, household items, and dairy.
- Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales (Stacked Column Chart): Compares outlet-wise sales contribution segmented by fat content.
- Total Sales by Outlet Establishment Year (Line Chart): Evaluates how outlet age impacts revenue trends over time.
- Sales by Outlet Size (Donut Chart): Analyzes the relationship between outlet size (Small, Medium, High) and total sales.
- Sales by Outlet Location (Funnel Chart): Assesses sales distribution across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 locations.
- All Metrics by Outlet Type (Matrix Table): Consolidated view of Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, Average Rating, and Item Visibility across outlet types.



9. Business Insights

- Regular fat products contribute higher overall sales compared to low-fat items
- Supermarket Type 1 outlets generate the highest revenue and item sales
- Medium-sized outlets show strong sales performance
- Tier 3 locations contribute the highest total sales
- Older outlets tend to show stable and consistent revenue trends
